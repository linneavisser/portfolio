Assignment 03.01.01 - Data collection

Use various research methods to collect data in regards to the main theme of basic UX, a t-shirt web shop, and to analyse and present the findings.

Process

I conducted primary and secondary by using the four research methods I was introduced to - desk, observation, interview and survey research. I collected data regarding t-shirt production, people wearing t-shirts in public, an interview about a generic t-shirt shop as well as a survey done with my fellow classmates mainly regarding t-shirt shopping behaviours.

I analysed the findings of the data collected and used some of these for the main assignment in this theme. The research I used was my desk research regarding the environmental impact of t-shirt production and findings from the survey where the majority of participants answered that they care about their environmental footprint when shopping for t-shirts.

Assignment 03.02.01 - Early prototypes

Develop our group’s initial ideas into a simple prototype designed with UX and UI conventions in mind.

Process

By using the four research methods I was introduced to - desk, observation, interview and survey research. I collected data regarding t-shirt production, people wearing t-shirts in public, an interview about a generic t-shirt shop as well as a survey done with my fellow classmates mainly regarding t-shirt shopping behaviours.

I analysed the findings of the data collected and used some of these for the main assignment in this theme, including my desk research regarding the environmental impact of t-shirt production and findings from the survey where the majority of participants answered that they care about their environmental footprint when shopping for t-shirts.

After deciding on which UX-problems to focus on we created a storyboard to develop a greater understanding of how our solution would solve these problems. Later we used card sorting to design the information architecture of our prototype and think-aloud tests on both our paper prototype and Xd prototype to see where our solution and user interface was succeeding and failing. The findings were then incorporated in the future iterations of our Xd prototype.

My Role

I was responsible for creating the style tile and designed the home page, about page, sizing page and half of the artists pages in our prototype. This included writing the copy for each of these pages, finding and editing relevant pictures. I conducted desk research regarding the pricing of producing the sort of t-shirts we proposed by comparing three similar companies with full price transparency and made the infographic regarding pricing used in the about page.

I implemented design principles when designing the Xd and infographic, focusing on adding contrast between colours and sizing in text as well as using gestalt laws. I also focused on the signal to noise ratio by making sure the information I added was relevant for the user.

I designed the illustrations for six of the twelve t-shirts in Illustrator and edited them onto the t-shirts in Photoshop.

Assignment 03.03.01 - Solution

To create a high-fidelity Xd prototype solving the UX-problems we chose to focus on.

Process

For the final iteration of our prototype we implemented the findings from our user testing; this included making the buttons bigger for easier accessibility, changing the images on the landing shopping page as the user was unsure if it was on the right page since we had pictures of the “artists” works and not t-shirts, adding more payment options to our checkout flow as well as adding back buttons to all pages for a better user experience.

We continued further developing the design of our webshop by changing some of the images, simplifying the layout and adding a Trustpilot logo to our home page to add credibility.
